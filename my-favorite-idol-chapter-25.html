Quick Summary

The main plotline of My Favorite Idol is the body-switch romance between Jooa and Yohan, who have to deal with the challenges and opportunities of living each other’s lives. They also have to face the dangers and secrets of the entertainment industry, as well as the jealousy and hatred of their rivals and enemies.

Along the way, they learn more about themselves and each other, and they grow as individuals and as a couple. The secondary plotlines include the mystery behind the body-switch phenomenon, the backstory and motivation of Jihyun, the rivalry and friendship between Yohan and his fellow idols, the bullying and redemption of Jooa and her classmates, and the family and personal issues of both Jooa and Yohan.
