Jessica Simpson Wedding Dress Designer: Wore Carolina Herrera Gown

May 16, 2024
blog

She took his breath away! Jessica Simpson married fiance Eric Johnson at the lavish San Ysidro Ranch in Montecito, Calif. on Saturday, July 5, and chose a wedding dress designer legendary for dramatic, elegant creations. Us Weekly can confirm that the fashion mogul walked down the aisle in a stunning custom-made Carolina Herrera gown on her special day.

Us previously broke news that Simpson, 33, would not be wearing white during her nuptials to the former NFL player, 34.

"Jessica is having something unique made," a source told Us at the time. "It will be very different from the Vera Wang she wore to wed Nick Lachey." (Simpson wore a white custom strapless beaded Vera Wang dress to wed the former 98 Degrees singer in 2002. They divorced four years later.)
