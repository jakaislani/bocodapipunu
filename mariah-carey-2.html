Mariah Carey also known as Mariah Angela Carey is an American singer, producer, actress and songwriter. As of 2022, Mariah Carey’s net worth is $350 million. She is referred to as the “Songbird Supreme” and the “Queen of Christmas” due to her five-octave vocal range, melismatic singing style, and characteristic usage of the whistle register.

She achieved prominence in 1990 with the release of her self-titled first album. From “Vision of Love” through “Emotions,” she was the first artist to have her first five songs chart at number one on the Billboard Hot 100.
