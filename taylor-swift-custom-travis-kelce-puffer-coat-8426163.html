Taylor Swift Took Her Game-Day Style to the Next Level With a Custom Travis Kelce Puffer Coat

August 22, 2024
blog

Not even subzero temperatures could stop Taylor Swift from cheering on Travis Kelce. Swift — who has quickly become fixture in the stands this NFL season — arrived at Arrowhead Stadium to watch the Kansas City Chiefs face off against the Miami Dolphins in the playoffs, and for the occasion, she upped the ante on her game-day style with a custom puffer coat that was fully dedicated to Kelce.

Repping her tight end boyfriend, Swift wore an oversized red jacket designed by fellow WAG Kristin Juszczyk, whose husband is the San Francisco 49ers fullback Kyle Juszczyk. Kelce's number 87 was emblazoned on the front and back of the jacket in white, as well as down one sleeve, while his last name was also featured on the back and the opposite sleeve.

Taylor wore a black base underneath, which consisted of a mock neck top that was tucked into a pair of straight-leg jeans, a leather belt with gold hardware, and combat boots complete with red soles. She sported her signature red lip and accessorized with a white beanie and red gloves.

The Chiefs ended up defeating the Dolphins 26-7, and following the game, Swift and Kelce were spotted making a cute exit from the stadium together. In a video shared to X, the couple were seen holding hands and chatting, trailing behind Patrick and Brittany Mahomes.
