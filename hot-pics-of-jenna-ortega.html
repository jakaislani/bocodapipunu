American actress Jenna Marie Ortega began her acting career as a child, earning recognition for her role as young Jane in the 2014–2019 season of The CW’s comedy-drama series Jane the Virgin.

She made her acting debut in the Disney Channel series Stuck in the Middle, for which she won an Imagen Award, in which she played Harley Diaz from 2016 to 2018. She appeared as Ellie Alves in the thriller series You’s second season in 2019 and starred in the Netflix family film Yes Day in 2021.

Following her performance in the teen drama The Fallout in 2021, Ortega went on to star in the slasher films X and Scream in 2022, establishing herself as a scream queen. Her performance in The Fallout was praised by critics. She was nominated for a Golden Globe Award for her performance as Wednesday Addams in the Netflix horror-comedy series Wednesday (2022).
