Fans frustrated over Fate: The Winx Saga

Netflix's drama Fate: The Winx Saga is inspired by the Nickelodeon animated series, Winx Club. The series follows the adventures of five fairies in training as they attend magical boarding school.

The five main casts are Bloom (Abigail Cowen), Stella (Hannah van der Westhuysen), Aisha (Precious Mustapha), Terra (Eliot Salt), and Musa (Elisha Applebaum), a mind fairy. So, why were fans angry?

The first trailer for Fate was released on December 10, 2020. Afterward, fans of the original Winx Club quickly took to social media to accuse the show of whitewashing key characters, Terra and Musa.

In the original cartoon, Musa is an East Asian, but Applebaum appears to be white. Meanwhile, Terra, played by white actor Salt, isn't in the original Winx Club. Season two of Fate might include more diverse characters.
