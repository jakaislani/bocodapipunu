➡ Atul Agnihotri Biography

Atul Agnihotri is an Indian film former actor, producer, and director. The actor started his Bollywood career as an actor and later he directed two films and achieved great success as a producer. Agnihotri’s first made a brief appearance as a child artist in the 1983 movie Pasand Apni Apni. His adult debut film career started with the 1993 Mahesh Bhatt movie Sir in which he acted alongside Pooja Bhatt while Naseeruddin Shah played a vital role in the film. It was the most notable film of his career and featured him as the lead protagonist. However, the actor did many fantastic performances and acted alongside Bollywood biggies. There is no doubt that he is considered among the most celebrated actors of Hindi cinema. Let us have a look at these unknown facts so that you can get to know more about him!

Agnihotri is the son-in-law of famous scriptwriter Salim Khan. He married producer and designer Alvira Khan from whom he has 2 children. Atul disappointed in his acting career and switched to direction with the film Dil Ne Jissay Apna Kaha.
